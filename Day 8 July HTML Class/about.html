About Sengeel Sneakers Store


Sengeel Sneakers Store is a Nigerian fashion sneakers and apparel corporation headquartered near Eleme, Port Harcourt, River State Nigeria. It is Nigeria's largest supplier of designer sneackers and apparel and a major manufacturer of sneakers materials, with revenue in excess of 46 billion Naira in its fiscal year 2023. It has notable brand ambassadors like Jack Grealish, Davido, Drake, Cristaino Ronaldo, Kevin Hart and Will Smith.
