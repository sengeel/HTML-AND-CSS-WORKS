Overview

TeenCoders is a school where children under the ages of 4 years to 18 years come to learn coding and coding related topics. It has different classes ranging from beginners to advanced level where you are grouped with people at your own level in order to make learning easier and faster. It cross across wide topics including CSS, HTML, JAVA and many more. It was founded in the 21st century to aid teenagers acquire the fundamental skills of coding in order for them to have knowledge of the IT gadgets around them. It is located at Artilery, Rumuogba Port Harcourt.It is among of the best coding schoool in Nigeria as it aims at providing good coding teaching services which has given it global recognition around the world. They have partnership with MIIT, MIT, Ontario Tech, Great Lakes Collegeand many more just to name a few. They have well-trained teachers and instructors.
