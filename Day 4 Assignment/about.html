About Sengeel Car Limited

Sengeel Car Limited is a Nigerian luxuury sports car manufacturer. The company was founded in 2007 by Mr Elvis Sengeel, a car industrialist, whose aim is to make sure that luxury cars are affordable and have long lasting warranty. The headquaters of Sengeel Car Limited is located in Lagos, Nigeria.
